Developing detailed CAD models of the servo-actuated booms and integrated solar sail system based on rigorous engineering analysis.

Meet Our Team

The engineers and scientists making solar sail innovation possible.

Nico Naranjo

Principal Investigator

Nico is an Aerospace Engineering major at the University of Texas, Austin.

As the Principal Investigator, Nico's role is to oversee technical development and research focus.

In his free time, Nico loves listening to music, updating his Letterboxd, trying local restaurants and coffee, and taking on any outdoor activity with his friends. A fun fact about Nico: he is red-green colorblind!

Annie Dmitrieff

Project Manager

Annie is an Astrophysics major at the University of Georgia.

As the Project Manager, Annie coordinates team efforts, manages timelines, and facilitates communication across all disciplines.

Annie also works with the University of Georgia's Center for Simulational Physics on simulational astrophysics research, and will be working with the MIT Haystack Observatory to detect stellar signatures from the early universe.

Elijah Guerrero

Business Lead

Elijah is a Management Information Systems (MIS) major at the University of Texas, Austin.

As the Business Administrator and Financier, Elijah manages project funding, budgeting, and external partnerships.

Elijah's favorite planet is Mars!

Rishab Shekhar

Mechanical Lead

Rishab is a Mechanical Engineering major at the University of Georgia.

As the Mechanical Lead, Rishab's role is to oversee the design, analysis, and integration of structural components to meet performance and reliability requirements.

Rishab also works with the UGA Small Satellite Research Lab and the American Institute of Aeronautics and Astronautics. He is interested in building things that go to space, propulsion, and simulation technologies.

Elyce Burce

Electrical Lead

Elyce is pursuing her Master's in Aerospace and Mechanical Engineering at Cornell University.

As an Electrical Lead, Elyce focuses on the design and integration of power systems, ensuring efficient energy distribution and actuator performance for the solar sail’s control mechanisms.

Elyce additionally works as an Electrical Design Engineer at L3Harris.

Rafael Perales

Electrical Lead

As an Electrical Lead, Rafael is responsible for circuit design and hardware implementation, working to optimize the performance and reliability of the sail’s actuation systems.
